# [<-](../README.md) Light Semolina Pudding

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                      |
|-------------|----------------------|
| 40g         | Erythritol           |
| 150ml       | Water                |
| 50g         | Casein protein powder|
| 30g         | Semolina             |
| 300ml       | 1.5% milk            |

## Method

### 1. Step
Combine the milk, erythritol, and semolina in a saucepan. Heat while stirring continuously.

### 2. Step
Semolina thickens in two stages, so continue stirring even after it starts to thicken. Once the mixture begins to bubble, reduce the heat. Make sure the mixture does not burn on the bottom of the pan.

### 3. Step
When the mixture stops thickening, remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool to about 60°C.

### 4. Step
In a separate bowl, mix the water and casein protein powder until fully combined.

### 5. Step
Once the semolina mixture has cooled, combine it with the casein-water mixture and stir until smooth.

## Remarks
- Do not cook the casein with the semolina mixture, or small hard lumps will form that cannot be dissolved.
