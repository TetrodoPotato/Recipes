# [<-](../README.md) Light Panna Cotta

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                              |
|-------------|------------------------------|
| 30g         | Erythritol                   |
| 0.04g       | Vanillin                     |
| 10g         | Inulin                       |
| 3g          | White gelatin (about 1 sheet)|
| 200ml       | 1.5% milk                    |

## Method
Heat to 80°C and let the inulin and gelatin dissolve.
