# [<-](../README.md) French Onion Soup

## Incredients
| Incredients |                            |
| ----------  | -------------------------- |
| 1kg         | Onions                     |
| 1 tsp       | White Sugar                |
| 800ml       | Beef stock                 |
| 2 tsp       | Thyme                      |
| 125ml       | Dry sherry or white wine   |
| 60g         | Butter                     |
| 2 tbs       | Olive oil                  |

## Method

### 1. Step
Chop the onions into strips of about 5mm.

### 2. Step
Melt the butter with the olive oil in a saucepan.

### 3. Step
Add the onions and white sugar. Fry over a low heat.\
Turn at least every 3 to 5 minutes to ensure even cooking.\
Continue until the onions are evenly browned.\
This can take up to an hour.

### 4. Step
Add the sherry or whine, mix and then add the beef stock and thyme.

### 5. Step
Leave to simmer for a further 30 to 40 minutes.
