# [<-](../README.md) French Onion Soup

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                            |
|-------------|----------------------------|
| 1kg         | Onions                     |
| 1 tsp       | White sugar                |
| 800ml       | Beef stock                 |
| 2 tsp       | Thyme                      |
| 125ml       | Dry sherry or white wine   |
| 60g         | Butter                     |
| 2 tbsp      | Olive oil                  |

## Method

### 1. Step
Chop the onions into strips about 5 mm wide.

### 2. Step
Melt the butter with the olive oil in a saucepan.

### 3. Step
Add the onions and white sugar. Fry over low heat.\
Turn at least every 3 to 5 minutes to ensure even cooking.\
Continue until the onions are evenly browned.\
This can take up to an hour.

### 4. Step
Add the sherry or wine, mix and then add the beef stock and thyme.

### 5. Step
Let simmer for another 30 to 40 minutes.
