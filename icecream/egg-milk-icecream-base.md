# [<-](../README.md) Egg Milk Ice Cream Base

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                         |
|-------------|-------------------------|
| 400ml       | Milk                    |
| 85g         | Sugar                   |
| 60g         | Egg yolks (about 3)     |

## Method

### 1. Step
Whisk milk, sugar, and egg yolks together in a saucepan until the mixture is smooth.

### 2. Step
Heat the mixture while stirring constantly until it reaches exactly 75°C (use a thermometer).\
Remove from the heat immediately, let it cool completely, then chill in the refrigerator and churn in an ice cream machine.

## Remarks
If the egg taste is very strong, the eggs were probably old, too much egg white got into the mixture, or the mixture was heated above 75°C.
