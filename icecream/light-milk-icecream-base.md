# [<-](../README.md) Light Milk Ice Cream Base

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                   |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 400ml       | Milk              |
| 80g         | Xylitol           |
| 0.5g        | Xanthan gum       |
| 1.0g        | Locust bean gum   |
| 1g          | Lecithin          |
| 10g         | Starch            |

## Method

### 1. Step
In a small bowl, mix together the xylitol, locust bean gum, lecithin, and xanthan gum.\
This dry-mixing step helps to prevent lumps when the powders are combined with the milk.

### 2. Step
Pour the milk into a stand blender and start blending at medium speed so that a stable vortex forms in the center.

### 3. Step
Slowly sprinkle the dry mixture into the vortex while the blender is running.\
Continue blending for at least 1 minute, until the milk starts to thicken slightly.

### 4. Step
Add the starch to the blender and keep mixing until the starch is fully dispersed and no visible lumps remain.

### 5. Step
Transfer the mixture to a saucepan. Heat gently while stirring and bring it up to about 85°C.

### 6. Step
Remove from the heat and let the mixture cool completely, stirring occasionally.\
As it cools, it should thicken and lightly gel.

### 7. Step
Churn the cooled base in an ice cream machine.

## Remarks
- Blend long enough after adding the dry mix so that all stabilizers hydrate properly.
- Do not boil vigorously; aim for controlled heating to 85°C to avoid scorching the milk.
