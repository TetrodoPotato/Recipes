# [<-](../README.md) Pretzel Breadstick

## Ingredients
| Ingredients |                           |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| 500g        | Wheat flour               |
| 250ml       | Water                     |
| 10g         | Salt                      |
| 7g          | Dry yeast                 |
| 1 tsp       | Sugar                     |
| 1g          | Sodium hydroxide*         |

*not natron. Its the food grade cleaning stuff.

## Method

### Step 1
Mix the water with the dry yeast and sugar. Stir until no lumps are visible.

### Step 2
Mix it with the flour and the salt and knead the mixture well for 10 to 15 minutes.\
Trust the process. At first, the dough seems to be too dry.

### Step 3
Leave the dough to rise in a warm place for 20 to 40 minutes.

### Step 4
Prepare a 5% sodium hydroxide solution by mixing 1g of sodium hydroxide with 20ml of water.\
Do not use aluminium or organic materials.

### Step 5
Knead the dough once more.\
Shape the dough into 8 even loaves of breadsticks.

### Step 6
Use a brush to cover each breadstick with the sodium hydroxide solution.\
Wear gloves and do not use any organic materials.

### Step 7
Place each stick evenly on a baking tray and cut into it.\
Let rise again in a warm place for about 40 minutes.\
The best place to do this is a slightly warmed oven at 40°C.\
Do not touch the breadsticks after that. You will squeeze out the air.\
The dough will not rise again in the oven.

### Step 8
Preheat the oven to 250°C.\
Put the breadsticks in the oven and pour a good amount of water on the bottom of the oven. Close the door quickly.\
Leave the breadsticks to bake for about 8 minutes.

### Step 9
After 8 minutes, pour in another batch of water and bake for another 4–6 minutes, or until the breadsticks have developed a deep brown color.

## Remarks
This produces even loaves without excessive oven spring. The baked breadsticks are sturdy and can be easily handled,
making them suitable for sandwiches or for slicing into small pieces.

To give them a crunchier and more unique appearance, you can reduce the second rise time to about 15-20 minutes.
